*
* CODING EXAMPLE 16.4.15
*
*
This coding example illustrates the use of the ALESERV and DSPSERV
macro instructions to remove the entry of a Data Space from the
access list and to delete the Data Space.


         ALESERV DELETE,ALET=DS01ALET
         DSPSERV DELETE,STOKEN=DS01STOK
         ...
DS01ALET DS    F    PROVIDED, RECEIVED FROM ALESERV-ADD MACRO
DS01STOK DS    D    PROVIDED, RECEIVED FROM DSPSERV-CREATE MACRO
