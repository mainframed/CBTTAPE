)ATTR DEFAULT(%+_)
)BODY
%TUTORIAL ---------------  MANUAL MANAGEMENT FACILITY  ---------------- TUTORIAL
%SELECTION ===>_ZCMD                                                           +
%
%                    --------------------------------------
                     |     MANUAL MANAGEMENT FACILITY     |
                     --------------------------------------
+
   The Manual Management Facility allows authorised users to Add, Update
   and Delete manuals on the LEB's Manual data base, and to update the
   list of copies held.

   It also allows users to Browse the list of manuals or to find a
   particular manual by subject or location.

   The following topics are presented in sequence, or may be selected by
   number:

  %1+ ADD                Add details of a manual
  %2+ UPDATE             Change details of a manual
  %3+ DELETE             Remove details of a manual
  %4+ BROWSE             Display a list of all manuals
  %5+ SEARCH             Display a list of selected manuals
  %6+ LOCATE             Display a list of copies of manuals
)PROC
     &ZSEL = TRANS( &ZCMD
                1,LMMPH020
                2,LMMPH120
                3,LMMPH060
                4,LMMPH040
                5,LMMPH080
                6,LMMPH090
                *,'?'
                )
)END
