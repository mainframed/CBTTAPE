         MACRO
&LABEL   #RTN
.*---------------------------------------------------------------------
.*
.*       STANDARD RETURN MACRO
.*
.*---------------------------------------------------------------------
         L     R13,4(R13)         GET BACK CHAIN POINTER
         ST    R15,16(R13)        SAVE REGISTER 15 (RETCODE)
         LM    R14,R12,12(R13)    RESTORE CALLERS REGS
         BR    R14                RETURN TO CALLER
