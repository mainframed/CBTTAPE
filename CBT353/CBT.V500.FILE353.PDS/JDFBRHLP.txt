%-----------------------JES2 DISPLAY FACILITY  (JDF)  --------------------------
+
+  This is the dataset display panel. Commands are:

   %D+nnn  Scroll down  "nnn" lines  (default is 20).

   %U+nnn  Scroll up    "nnn" lines  (default is 20).

   %R+     Scroll right.

   %L+     Scroll left.

   %T+     Scroll to the top.

   %B+     Scroll to the bottom.

   %F+ccc  Find character string "ccc". "F" with no operands is repeat.

   %E+     End.
)END
