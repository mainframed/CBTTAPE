      The%Tutorial Print+option sends hard copies of tutorial panels to
   a remote printer.  Tutorials are printed in the order that they would
   be displayed by the ISPF Tutorial facility.  Several print options
   are available and output can be sent to a Xerox 2700 Laser Printer or
   a standard IBM printer.

      The following topics are presented in sequence, or may be selected
   by number.

     %1+- General information
     %2+- Entering ISPF panel library names
     %3+- Entering "other" partitioned data set
     %4+- Top tutorial ID
     %5+- Print options
     %6+- Restrictions

)PROC
     &ZUP   = EDPCT003
     &ZSEL  = TRANS(&ZCMD
                  1,TUTPT010
                  2,TUTPT021
                  3,TUTPT031
                  4,TUTPT080
                  5,TUTPT040
                  6,TUTPT070
                  )
)END
