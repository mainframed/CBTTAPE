RXWAIT - REXX function to wait whole or fractions of seconds. May also
be used as a TSO command and a batch pgm

Description of members:

 RXWAIT    Product, edit and run this member.
 RXWTDOC   Documentation.
 RXWTHIST  Product history.
 RXWTIVPx  IVPs
 RXWTMACS  Local macros.
 SYSMACS   ASM copy book containing combined non-IBM macros.
 REXXMACS  ASM copy book containing my macros for interfacing with REXX.
 WSAMMACS  ASM copy book containig my structured assembler macro set.
           The macros are equivalent but not identical to IBM's macros.
