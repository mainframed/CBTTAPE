.cm SCRIPT Reference CB
.SUBT Purpose
The COLUMN BEGIN control word causes subsequent text to begin at the
top of a new column.
.SUBT Format
|
|  .CB
|
.SUBT Usage
When in multiple column mode, this control word causes
the following text to appear at the top of the next output column.
If this control word is encountered while processing
the last column on a page then it is equivalent to a page eject.
.sp
The same action may be caused by a CONDITIONAL COLUMN BEGIN (.CC).
.SUBT Defaults
This control word causes a break.
No operand may be specified.
.SUBT Notes
.of 4
(1) This control word is illegal in a Keep or Footnote.
.cm End of CB
