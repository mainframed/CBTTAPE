.cm SCRIPT REFERENCE EP
.SUBT Purpose
The EVEN PAGE EJECT control word causes output to continue on an
even numbered page.
.SUBT Format
|
|  .EP
|
.SUBT Usage
The .EP control word causes one or two page ejects so that output
continues on an even numbered page.
.SUBT Defaults
This control word creates a break.
.SUBT Notes
.of 4
(1) If empty-page suppression is on (.EM NO), the page number will be
incremented to the next even number and output
will continue on the next physical page.
.sp 1
.of 4
(2) The EVEN PAGE EJECT control has the same effect as the
EVEN PAGE (.PV) control.
.cm End of EP
