Output of command:  TSUB E2M    (like E2L except the new length
                                 only includes ONE blank at the end.)

     This command measures the length of the IKJEFTE2 table in
     the user's address space, to (and including) the first blank
     table entry.  Then this length is plugged into the LWA field
     which is supposed to contain the length of the IKJEFTE2 table.
     The messages below, tell you what happened.  Another TSUB E2D
     command will tell you the resulting state of the E2 table.
