This HASP exit 5 processes the following installation JES2 commands:

1. $REPEXIT - Replace a JES2 exit module.  The exit environment may be
              "JES2", "SUBTASK", "USER", or "FSS".
2. $ADDEXIT - Add a JES2 exit module.  The exit environment may be
              "JES2", "SUBTASK", "USER", or "FSS".
