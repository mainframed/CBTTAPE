         MACRO
&NAME    SUBLR &R,&S
.*       PDSDOC *RELOAD SUBIN REGISTER........................(M56035)* SUBLR
.*
.*       LOAD REGISTER FROM PREVIOUS SUBIN SAVE AREA ( WITHIN ROUTINE )
.*             SUBLR RX
.*             SUBLR RX,RY
.*
         GBLA  &SUBINC             COMMON TO SUBIN/SUBOUT/SUBLR
         LCLC  &P,&PP
&P       SETC  '&R'
         AIF   ('&S' EQ '').A01
&P       SETC  '&S'
.A01     ANOP
&PP      SETC  '$&SUBINC'
&NAME    L     &R,&PP&P ¬ê          LOAD REGISTER FROM SUBIN SAVE AREA
         MEND
