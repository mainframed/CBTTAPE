*   PDS GLOBALS SUBCOMMAND LISTING TABLE  -  S.GOLOB - 11/88       *
*                                                                  *
*                                                                  *
* GENERAL DESCRIPTION:  SUPPORT OF A PDS SUBCOMMAND TO LIST        *
*                        THE SETTINGS OF THE PDS GLOBAL VARIABLES  *
*                        AT THE TIME OF ASSEMBLY.                  *
*                                                                  *
*                       SOME OF THESE VARIABLES CAN BE CHANGED     *
*                        DURING PDS EXECUTION.  THOSE SETTINGS     *
*                        CAN BE DISPLAYED WITH OTHER SUBCOMMANDS,  *
*                        SUCH AS SETML, SETLOG, ETC.               *
*                                                                  *
*                                                                  *
* TABLE STRUCTURE:                                                 *
*                                                                  *
*      EACH TABLE ENTRY HAS 64 BYTES, EXCEPT FOR END-OF-TABLE,     *
*        WHICH CONSISTS OF 2 BYTES, X'FFFF'.                       *
*                                                                  *
*      EACH ENTRY IS DIVIDED INTO 4 PARTS.                         *
*        A)  CL8   -  NAME OF THE GLOBAL VARIABLE.                 *
*        B)  CL8   -  DEFAULT VALUE OF THE GLOBAL VARIABLE         *
*        C)  CL40  -  SHORT ENGLISH DESCRIPTION OF WHAT THE        *
*                         VARIABLE DOES, OR SETS.                  *
*        D)  CL8   -  VALUE OF THE GLOBAL VARIABLE AT THE          *
*                         TIME WHEN THIS PDS LOAD MODULE           *
*                         WAS ASSEMBLED.                           *
*                                                                  *
*      THERE IS ONE SPECIAL TYPE OF HEADER ENTRY.                  *
*        A)  HEADER ENTRIES - TWO TABLE ENTRIES AT LABEL           *
*              "GLOBHDR", WHICH CONTAIN THE TITLE FOR THE          *
*              DISPLAY.                                            *
*                                                                  *
*                                                                  *
********************************************************************

*                             -------------
