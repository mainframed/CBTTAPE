)F FUNCTION -

The DAPF command will display the datasets in the running systems
APF Authorized list. The list includes the volume serial of where
the dataset is located. This list is obtained from the internal
control blocks and is good for the life of this IPL, or until
someone does a SET PROG=xx operator command.
)X SYNTAX -
     DAPF

ALIAS    - NONE
REQUIRED - (none..there are no operands to the command)


)O OPERANDS -
   There are no operands to the DAPF command.
