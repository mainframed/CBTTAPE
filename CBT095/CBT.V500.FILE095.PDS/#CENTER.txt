%EDITMAC  -------------------- CENTER MACRO  -------------------------  TUTORIAL
%COMMAND ===>_ZCMD                                                             +
+
+THE%CENTER+MACRO IS USED TO CENTER TEXT WITHIN A DATASET.
+USE:
+FIRST ON THE LINE NUMBERS OF THE DATA  SET  INDICATE A RANGE OF THE LINES TO BE
+"CENTERED"  , "CC"  TO "CC"  OR "CNN"   (NN BENG NUMBERS).  THEN ON THE COMMAND
+LINE TYPE IN CENTER.  (SCROLLING  IS  NOT  ALLOWED AFTER TYPING IN CENTER UNTIL
+THE CENTER COMMAND IS COMPLETE).   ADDITIONAL  PARAMETERS OF LEFT AND RIGHT MAY
+BE ENTERED TO LIMIT THE COLUMNS IN  WHICH  TO CENTER.  IF LEFT IS ENTERED RIGHT
+MUST ALSO BE ENTERED OR THE RIGHT BOUNDS WILL BE THE DEFAULT.
+
%COMMAND ===> CENTER {LEFT RIGHT}
+***************************** TOP OF DATA *************************************
+000001  DATA LINE 1
+CC0002  DATA LINE 2
+CC0003  DATA LINE 3
+
+RESULT:
+
+000001  DATA LINE 1
+000002                              DATA LINE 2
+000003                              DATA LINE 3
)PROC
&KEY = .PFKEY
)END
