EDITMAC ---------------------- PRTIT     MACRO -------------------------TUTORIAL
+
+     THE PRTIT MACRO WILL INVOKE VPSPRINT TO PRINT THE DATASET YOU ARE
+     EDITING.
+
+
%USE:
+
+           ENTER%PRTIT+ ON  THE  EDIT  COMMAND  LINE  WHEN YOU HAVE
+           COMPLETED EDITING THE  DATASET.   YOU  CAN  SUPPLY UP TO
+           FOUR (4) FURTHER VPSPRINT OPTIONS. THE FIST OPTION MUST
+           BE THE TARGET PRINTER.
+
+           ALL VPSPRINT SUBCOMMANDS ARE SUPPORTED, BUT ONLY 4.
+
+
%EXAMPLE:+
+
+ COMMAND ===> PRTIT PRT2 FCB(P301) NOHEADER ASC
+
)PROC
&KEY = .PFKEY
)END
