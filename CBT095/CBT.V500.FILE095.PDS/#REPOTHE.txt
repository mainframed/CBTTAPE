%EDITMAC  ------------------ REPOTHER  MACRO  ------------------------  TUTORIAL
%COMMAND ===>_ZCMD                                                             +
+
+THE%REPOTHER+MACRO IS USED TO CREATE/REPLACE  A DATASET OR PDS MEMBER
+OTHER THAN THE CURRENT PDS BEING EDITED. REPOTHER MUST BE USED FROM
+WITHIN A PDS.
%RESTRICTION: THE IBM "COPY" MODULE (IKJEBCY1) MUST BE IN THE LINKLIST
%FOR REPOTHER TO WORK.
+REPOTHER COPIES THE ENTIRE MEMBER IN EDIT AT THE TIME, RANGE
+COMMANDS ARE NOT NEEDED, AND NO OTHER PANELS ARE NEEDED TO IDENTIFY
+THE TARGET DATASET.
+
+USE:
+TYPE%REPOTHER+ON THE COMMAND LINE FOLLOWED BY THE TARGET NAME
+TO CREATE/REPLACE.  (THIS COMMAND WILL CREATE A NEW DATASET IF NEEDED)
+
%COMMAND ===> REPOTHER 'THE.TARGET.DATASET'
+***************************** TOP OF DATA *************************************
)PROC
&KEY = .PFKEY
)END
