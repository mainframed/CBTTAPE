**  COMMON CALL CONVENTION:                                          **
**                                                                   **
**  SUBCALL SUBINST,(@IN,@OUT),VL,MF=(E,CALLPARM)                    **
**                                                                   **
**  WHERE @IN IS THE ADDRESS OF SIX BYTE TEXT TO BE DECODED          **

**   15-35    ASSEMBLER EQUIVALENT                                   **
**                                                                   **
**  NOTE: USES CALLER'S SAVE AREA (R13) FOR WORKING STORAGE          **
**                                                                   **
**  RETURN CODE:                                                     **

**    R15 = 4    NOT AN INSTRUCTION R0 - LENGTH IF IT WERE VALID     **
**    R15 = 20   ERROR IN PARM LIST                                  **
**                                                                   **
***********************************************************************
