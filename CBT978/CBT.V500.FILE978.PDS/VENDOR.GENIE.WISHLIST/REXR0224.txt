NAssignment
N----------
NA single clause of the form symbol=expression is an instruction known
Nas an assignment. An assignment gives a variable a (new) value.
N
NExamples:
N---------
D  /* assignment */
D  max_width = 132
D
D  heading.1 = Center("My sample REXX example",max_width," ")
* End of WISH.
