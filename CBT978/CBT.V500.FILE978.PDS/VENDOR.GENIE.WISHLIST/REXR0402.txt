NBuilt-in functions
N------------------
NABS
N-------
Nreturns the absolute value of number. The result has no sign and is
Nformatted according to the current NUMERIC settings.Removes the SIGN,
Nturning it neither negative nor positive.
N
N
NSyntax:
D  ABS(number)
N
N
NExample
N-------
I  SAY ABS("12.3") "-> 12.3"
I  SAY ABS(" -0.307") "-> 0.307"
* End of WISH.
