NDefinition
N----------
NArithmetic operators
N--------------------
NREXX arithmetic is performed by the operators +, -, *, /, %, //,
Nand ** (add, subtract, multiply, divide, integer divide, remainder,
Nand power), which all act on two terms, and the prefix plus and minus
Noperators, which both act on a single term. This section describes the
Nway in which these operations are carried out.
