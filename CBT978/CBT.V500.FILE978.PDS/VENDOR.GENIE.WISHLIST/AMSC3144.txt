NCase 14: Destaging All Modified Data in Cache and NVS to DASD
N-------------------------------------------------------------
NSETCACHE DESTAGE specifies that all modified data in cache and NVS is
Nscheduled for destage to DASD. This command is usually issued for
Nshutdown and is included in the operator command Halt End of Day
N(Halt EOD).
