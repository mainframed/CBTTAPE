NChapter 10. TSO/E REXX commands
N-------------------------------
NHE
N--
NHE (Halt Execution) is an immediate command you can use to halt the
Nexecution of a REXX exec. The HE immediate command is available only
Nif an exec is running in TSO/E and you press the attention interrupt
Nkey to enter attention mode. You can enter HE in response to the REXX
Nattention prompting message, IRX0920I.
N
NSyntax
D  HE
