NBuilt-in functions
N------------------
NFORM
N----
NFORM returns the current setting of NUMERIC FORM. See the NUMERIC
Ninstruction for more information.
N
N
NSyntax:
D  FORM()
N
N
NExample
N-------
I  SAY "FORM()"
I  current_form = Form()
I  SAY "The current form is" current_form"."
