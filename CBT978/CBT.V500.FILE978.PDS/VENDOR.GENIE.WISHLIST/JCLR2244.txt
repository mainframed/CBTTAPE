NMAILBCC Parameter
N-----------------
NUse the MAILBCC parameter to specify one or more e-mail addresses of
Nthe blind copy (bcc) recipients of an e-mail. A bcc means that other
Nrecipients of the e-mail do not see the bcc recipient listed.
