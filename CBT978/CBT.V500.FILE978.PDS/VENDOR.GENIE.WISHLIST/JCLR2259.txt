NOUTPUT JCL Statement
N====================
NOVERLAYF Parameter
N------------------
NSpecifies to place the named medium overlay on the front side of each
Nsheet to print.
N
D//outds OUTPUT OVERLAYF=ovlyname
I                 ovlyname: Specifies the medium overlay name, where
I                   the overlay name is 1 though 8 alphanumeric or
I                   national ($, #, @) characters and the first of those
I                   characters is alphabetic or national.
